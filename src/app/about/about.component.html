Get to know us

Proudly Rocking Since 2022

Our festival is a vibrant celebration of culture and music. With a packed schedule of concerts, workshops, sports activities, and a beautiful lake for swimming, there's always something exciting to do.

The music spans rock, folk, funk, and ska, featuring live performances by local bands. The eastern part honors Soviet-era bardic traditions with acoustic guitar music, while the center stage blends nostalgic Russian rock, Balkan beats, and classic Soviet art-rock from the 1990s.

Wandering through the festival fosters spontaneous interactions and diverse community connections.

Attendees from various countries, including Russia and Ukraine, come together in a harmonious convergence of people and cultures, creating an unforgettable tapestry of music, workshops, and activities.
